Visiting Students

We are seeking highly motivated visiting students to work on WiFi scheduling algorithms for Generative AI services. These positions are open to current Master’s or Ph.D. students for a duration of up to one year, with an expected start in August or September 2026. Full financial support will be provided for the entire visit, with a stipend above the standard China Scholarship Council (CSC) level.

Applicants are expected to have research experience in network optimization, with publications in IEEE conferences or journals (e.g., GLOBECOM, ICC, WCNC, TCOM, TWC, TVT). Preference will be given to candidates with NS-3 for network simulation, and familiarity with or strong interest in LLM inference systems (e.g., vLLM, DeepSpeed, TensorRT-LLM).

Interested candidates can email shugang_hao@alumni.sutd.edu.sg with CV attached. Please use the subject line Visiting Student – WiFi for GenAI.
